01

Overview

FCOMPANION is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the FCOMPANION service — an AI-powered chatbot for students of the Faculty of Computing, Nile University of Nigeria.

By using FCOMPANION, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

02

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Info First name, last name, email, level, department Registration & personalisation
Credentials Password (hashed, never plain text) Authentication
Chat History Messages sent to and received from the chatbot Persistent chat experience
Session Data JWT tokens, session identifiers Secure access management
Profile Picture Optional photo you upload Profile personalisation

We do not collect your device location, contacts, microphone data, or any information outside of what is listed above.

03

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery — to authenticate you and provide chatbot responses
  • Personalisation — to address you by name and tailor responses to your department and level
  • Chat continuity — to store and display your conversation history across sessions
  • Security — to verify your identity via OTP during sign-up and password reset
  • Service improvement — anonymised, aggregated usage patterns may be reviewed to improve the chatbot's accuracy

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the FCOMPANION service.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using the following measures:

  • Database — MongoDB Atlas with SSL/TLS encryption in transit
  • Passwords — Hashed using bcrypt before storage; never stored in plain text
  • Authentication — JWT tokens with expiration; stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • OTP codes — Temporary, expire after a short window, and are invalidated after use
  • API — Hosted on Render with HTTPS enforced on all endpoints

No system is 100% secure. While we implement strong safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited cases:

  • Google Dialogflow — Your chat messages are processed by Dialogflow (Google Cloud) to generate AI responses. Google's data processing terms apply. Messages are not stored by Google beyond the processing window.
  • Resend (Email Service) — Your email address is shared with Resend solely for the purpose of delivering OTP verification emails.
  • Legal obligation — We may disclose your data if required by applicable law or a valid legal process.

Beyond the above, your data is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third party.

06

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data — Retained until you request deletion or your account is terminated
  • Chat history — Retained indefinitely unless you use the "Clear Chat" feature or request deletion
  • OTP codes — Automatically deleted after use or expiry (typically within 10 minutes)
  • Inactive accounts — Accounts with no activity for 12 months may be subject to deletion following a notice email

Upon account deletion, all personally identifiable data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

07

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update inaccurate or incomplete information via the Profile page
  • Deletion — Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Request your chat history in a readable format
  • Objection — Object to specific uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10.

08

Cookies & Local Storage

FCOMPANION does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Your authentication token (JWT)
  • Your name and basic profile details for display purposes
  • Your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Your Dialogflow session ID for chat continuity

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to third parties. You can clear it at any time by logging out or clearing your browser's local storage.

09

Minors

FCOMPANION is intended for university students and is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately so we can remove their data.
